重载.bashrc:
source ~/.bashrc
简版： . ~/.bashrc

=========================
（ubuntu限定）
在当前路径下，创建一个通往其它（任何）路径的symbolic link:
ln -s /home/ubuntu/pms myfolder

=========================
to see if any process is bound to port 8000:
sudo netstat -pna | grep 8000
