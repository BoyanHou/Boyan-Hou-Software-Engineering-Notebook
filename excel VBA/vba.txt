在control panel内 （！不是常规的系统设置里，必须是control panel内）设置地区，以应对excel & vba中文乱码问题：
1. Open Control Panel
2. Click Region
3. Click the Administrative tab
4. Click the Change system locale button
5. Select which language to use when displaying text in programs
